Biography

My parents are from Colombia, but I was born and raised in South Florida. I went to an arts middle school, and then an arts high school. I am currently attending Tufts University and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston, Massachussetts.

At Tufts, my studies lie between a bit of both worlds. Here, I spend part of my week at the School of the Museum of Fine Arts working on my BFA in Fine Art, and the rest of my time at Tufts University working on my BS in Engineering Psychology. Currently, I'm interested in cognitively designed websites that are also aesthetically pleasing. Bear with me as I learn to actually make them...
